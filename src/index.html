About Us

A family run establishment with a passion for authentic produce and excellent food.
We use original Italian products, serve Italian wine and delicious Italian coffee. You can eat fresh seafood, fresh fish, vegetables and meat. Here you can try home-made Italian cuisine.
Ristorante Tesoro is a truly Italian restaurant. The restaurant has a family atmosphere, we serve and cook for our customers. This is your "little Italia".
